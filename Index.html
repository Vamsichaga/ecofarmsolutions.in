How can I make a garden in a less space?

The only answer is Vertical Farming, what is vertical farming?? Do you love gardening, but do not have enough space in your house to maintain a garden? Then a vertical garden may be the solution to your problem. Vertical gardening is not only a popular trend these days, but it also has aesthetic, physiological, economic and environmental benefits. A vertical garden saves your space. . A vertical garden helps you to plant a variety of plants. A vertical garden helps to grow more types of plants.

My terrace is so spacious how can I make this place useful?

Having a terrace or roof garden gives you direct access to fresh vegetables and fruits. Nothing tastes improved then food cooked vegetables plucked straight off the plants,or eaten in their freshest form. Advantages of Terrace Gardening: First benefit is the reduction of indoor temperature by 6- 8 degree, thereby it can lower air conditioning cost. Lowering overall heat absorption of buildings and insulate the building against heat and cold Enabling consumption of pesticide-free, healthy green and fresh vegetables Conducive to a routine physical exercise activity, health improvement due to clean air and closer association with the nature Enhancement of oxygen percentage in the air Reduction of sound pollution Acting as a habitat for city-weary birds An Ideal area to exercise and inhale better quality air every day

How can I get a fresh air in my home?

Now-a-days pollution is increasing a lot so, the remedy is indoor gardening. Indoor gardening relieves stress, boosts creativity, productivity, and focus, and promotes recovery. There's some evidence that houseplants may positively influence the air quality in your home as well. It's important to know which plants are toxic if you have children or pets in the home.

Chemicals are polluting the nature and us too. what is the solution for it??

The only solution is to use organic pests in farming.Organic pest and disease management is a method of controlling pests without using chemicals. Instead, other insects, birds, animals, plants or mechanical techniques are used. The benefits of chemical pesticides decrease over time as pests become resistant to them.
